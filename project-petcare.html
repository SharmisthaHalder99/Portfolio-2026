Process

From Research to Relief

01

Mapped daily medication habits

Interviewed caregivers and elderly users to understand how medication habits fit into the rhythm of daily life — meals, bedtime, morning routines — and built the reminder system around those anchors.

02

Simplified the dashboard

Prioritised one-screen visibility so important health signals — today's medications, missed doses, upcoming refills — are understood at a glance without any navigation needed.

03

Validated with real users

Conducted accessibility testing with target users aged 60+, validating contrast ratios, touch target sizes, and type scales against WCAG AA — iterating until zero critical errors remained.
