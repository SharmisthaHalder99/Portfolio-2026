Process

Designing the Brand's Mobile Voice

01

Translated brand identity to mobile

Worked with Twilla's brand guidelines to define a mobile-first visual language — type scale, colour palette, spacing tokens — that preserved the brand's elegance on a 390px screen.

02

Mapped discovery journeys

Defined three distinct user modes — explorer, intent buyer, wishlist builder — and designed navigation flows that serve all three without cluttering the primary surface.

03

Polished every micro-interaction

Added subtle animations, swipe gestures, and haptic cue opportunities to the prototype — details that elevate the experience from clean to genuinely luxurious.
